Accessed via a staircase from the main eating area and with a 50-seater capacity, our function room is suitable for use as a corporate meeting place, for wedding receptions, parties, wakes or other celebrations / functions. Having its own fully-equipped corner bar, independent ladies and gents WCs and a superb roof terrace, the space is ideal for a variety of occasions.

If you would like to discuss your requirements further or to check availability, please do not hesitate to get in touch, either by telephone (01983 825501) or via email: info@theeightbells.net

Please note that due to its location on the first floor the function room is not accessible to wheelchair users.
