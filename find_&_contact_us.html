The Eight Bells is located in the village of Carisbrooke just a short distance west of the island's premier town of Newport.
Situated in the main High Street, the pub is easy to find and can be accessed on foot or by road

By Bus


The island's bus service is run by South Vectis. Carisbrooke High Street is served by a number of different routes.


From West Wight and Newport Bus Station: Routes 7, 12 or 38


If travelling from town or villiage located in the south, North, East of the island you should firstly travel to Newport then proceed to Carisbrooke, either on foot, or continuing by bus.
